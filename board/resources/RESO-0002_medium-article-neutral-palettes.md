# The Secret to Creating Neutral Color Palettes

By Pablo Figueiredo (Jan 2023). The article that identified the parabolic relationship between lightness and perceived chroma, and proposed modelling saturation as a lightness-dependent curve to produce tinted neutrals with consistent perceived intensity.

Core insight: in a saturation/lightness plane, the perceptual boundary between "grey" and "chromatic" follows a parabolic curve — not a straight line. Colors at lightness extremes need more saturation to register as tinted, while midtones need less.
